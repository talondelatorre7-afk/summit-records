WE BUILD ARTIFACTS, NOT CONTENT.

The industry is obsessed with algorithms, 15-second hooks, and rapid-fire releases. Summit Records was engineered as a counter-culture to the mainstream machine.

We are looking for architects of sound. If your audio pushes boundaries, defies traditional genres, and refuses to conform, we provide the infrastructure to distribute it globally without demanding ownership of your art.

TRANSPARENCY (FAQ)

DO I REALLY KEEP 90%?

Yes. You keep 90% of all royalties generated from the tracks distributed through us. The 10% covers the lifetime backend management and placement of your audio.

IS THERE AN UPFRONT FEE?

No. We operate strictly on the backend split. We don't charge submission fees, upload fees, or maintenance fees.

WHAT GENRES DO YOU ACCEPT?

Our anchor artist operates in Christian Experimental Pop, but we are open to any non-mainstream, avant-garde, or strictly underground sounds. If it sounds like commercial radio, it's not for us.
